About Me

I create websites that help set you apart from the competition. My background in digital marketing, copywriting and design lends itself well to creating clean, user-friendly experiences. My specialties include HTML5, CSS, WordPress, JavaScript and UX.

Web development is more than a 9 to 5 job for me. It's a passion. And to ensure I consistently exceed my clients' expectations, I make it a habit to stay up to date on current design trends, development techniques and best practices.

When I’m not coding, you’ll find me exploring new places or hiking and camping in the mountains with my dog, Bear. I’m a podcast junkie who appreciates all things outdoors and loves a good nonfiction book with a glass of wine.
